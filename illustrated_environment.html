Original Beach Photo

Illustrated Version

For this illustrated environment assignment, I picked a picture of a beach environment. Beaches make sense visually as they are not too cluttered and can be broken up into simple shapes. Elements of any beach environment can be broken up into three sections: sky, ocean, and sand. I picked this image because I wanted to visualize a beach with its shapes and colors. I first started off sketching out large rectangles of color that would fill my entire image. I colored the sky light blue, ocean dark blue and sand a tan color. Looking at this image, it is broken down into geometric shapes which helps me understand how digital artists can create lifelike images from something so simple.
