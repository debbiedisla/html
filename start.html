The Last Train Home

You leave your night class and enter the subway station. It’s later than normal and there is no one on the platform. No MTA workers, no rush hour of people; nothing. The flickering lights above you cast shadows on the walls, and an odd feeling surrounds your stomach.

You reach for your phone, but it just died. You look up to see the sign flashing over the tracks that clearly reads NO TRAINS. Then you hear it… the sound of something coming from far away.

What do you do?

  • Remain in the station
  • Exit station back outside
