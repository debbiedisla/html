The Last Train Home

Narrative

Story is about a student that is leaving her class at nighttime. When she enters the subway station something weird starts happening. MTA workers are nowhere to be seen and station is nearly empty. Lights are flickering, her phone dies and train appears when it says NO TRAINS. Mood of the story is mysterious and suspenseful but not cheesy. Every place that the character at someday appears to be normal.

You will decide what she does. Do you wait in the station or leave, follow the weird noise or avoid it, and take the mysterious train or call someone for help. There will be branching of the story that could lead to multiple endings. Ending(s) could be safe and she gets home or mysterious where she takes the wrong train.
